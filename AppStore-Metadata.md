**Description**
```
Dhikr keeps remembrance present through your day — quietly, without getting in the way.

It lives in your Mac's menu bar (no Dock icon, no window to manage) and, at the interval you choose, slips a single adhkar into the corner of your screen. A few seconds later it fades on its own. No notifications to clear, no feed to scroll.

FEATURES
• Gentle reminders on your schedule — every 15, 30, 60, or 120 minutes, or any interval from 1 to 240.
• Morning & evening adhkar, on time — turn on time-of-day matching and Dhikr favors morning remembrances in the morning and evening ones toward Maghrib.
• 20 built-in adhkar from well-known tradition — with transliteration, English translation, and source.
• Make it yours — add your own adhkar, edit any of them, and choose exactly which are enabled.
• Tasbeeh counter — tap the card to count your repetitions (33×, 100×, or your own).
• Your corner, your screen — pick which corner it appears in, how long it stays, the font size, and which display on a multi-monitor setup.
• Optional sound — a soft chime when a reminder appears, or keep it silent.
• Launch at login — always there when you start your Mac.
• Arabic & English — full right-to-left interface, switchable instantly.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Dhikr has no account, no sign-in, and no network. Nothing you do leaves your Mac. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection of any kind — your remembrances and settings stay on your device.

Dhikr is free.
```

## ⚠️ Build blockers (not metadata — fix before upload succeeds)
`project.yml` is set for local/ad-hoc builds; the Mac App Store will reject it as-is:
- Set a real `DEVELOPMENT_TEAM` and `CODE_SIGN_STYLE: Automatic` (or Apple Distribution signing).
- `ENABLE_HARDENED_RUNTIME: YES`.
- Add **App Sandbox** entitlement (`com.apple.security.app-sandbox = YES`) — mandatory. Current features (menu bar, launch-at-login via SMAppService, sound, screen positioning) all work sandboxed; **no** network entitlement needed.
- Archive in Xcode → Distribute App → App Store Connect.
